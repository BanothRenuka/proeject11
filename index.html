HR Policy Document

Effective Date: 01 January 2026

1. Leave Policy

The startup follows a structured leave policy to support employee well-being while ensuring uninterrupted business operations. Employees are encouraged to plan their leaves in advance and obtain approval from their reporting managers.

  • Employees are entitled to 12 days of paid casual leave per year.
  • 8 days of sick leave can be availed with proper intimation.
  • Leaves should be applied through official communication channels.
  • Unused leave cannot be carried forward without approval.

2. Code of Conduct

The organization expects all employees to maintain professionalism, integrity, and ethical behavior at all times.

  • Employees must treat colleagues with respect and dignity.
  • Discrimination or harassment of any form is strictly prohibited.
  • Company resources should be used responsibly.
  • Confidential company information must not be disclosed.

3. Work From Home (WFH) Guidelines

Work-from-home is permitted based on job role, performance, and project requirements. Employees working remotely are expected to maintain productivity and accountability.

  • Employees must be available during standard working hours.
  • Reliable internet connection is mandatory.
  • Daily progress updates must be shared with managers.
  • WFH approval is subject to management discretion.
